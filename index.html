About Me

Hi! I’m Aishwarya Ravi, a web developer and cybersecurity enthusiast currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Kristu Jayanti College (2023–2026).

I’m diving deeper into cybersecurity with a strong interest in ethical hacking, network protection, and vulnerability analysis.

Fun fact: I never miss a chance to travel or take up new leadership opportunities—both help me grow as a curious, connected creator.
