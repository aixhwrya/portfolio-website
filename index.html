About Me

Hello! I’m Aishwarya Ravi, a web developer and aspiring cybersecurity analyst who finds joy in solving problems with code and compassion. I have over 3 years of experience building responsive and secure web applications using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and MySQL.

I’m a national-level volleyball player and a proud alumna of St. Xavier’s Sr. Sec. School, Jaipur. Sports have taught me teamwork, leadership, and resilience—qualities I carry into every project.

Outside of tech, I actively volunteer with NGOs to teach and uplift underprivileged communities. I strongly believe in Tech for Good and use my skills to contribute to initiatives that make an impact.

Currently, I’m diving deeper into cybersecurity—exploring secure coding practices, log analysis, and system vulnerabilities while continuously learning through online labs and projects.
